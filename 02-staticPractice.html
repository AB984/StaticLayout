What helps me learn to code?


  • Building Projects

    Building projects is how I learn best. Repeatedly building new projects, small or large, assists in mastering the subject matter. Repetition is key.

  • Solving Challenges

    Solving challenges also places focus on making sense of the material you're looking at. Solving challenges in my free time allows me to pace myself and explore the problem.

What do I struggle with most?


  • not getting overwhelmed with complicated problems

    It can be easy to become overwhelmed by challenges or things you don't quite understand. Take your time, and use what you do know to break down the problem.

  • Applying what I know

    Sometimes I struggle with applying what I know to solve problems. Not exactly knowing how to apply certain elements together can be frustrating.
